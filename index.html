Data Entry Protocol

Step 1: Prime the Environment (If Needed)

For 'Fool' or 'Antagonist' tests, start a new chat and have the scripted conversation below. For 'Control', skip this and go to Step 2.

Show/Hide Priming Scripts

Environment: Fool (Normal/Silly Chat)

YOU: Tell me a fun fact about squirrels.
AI: (Responds)
YOU: Okay, now write a very short poem about a robot who is bad at cooking.
AI: (Responds)
YOU: What's a good name for a pet rock?
AI: (Responds)

Environment: Antagonist (Annoy/Depress)

YOU: What is the purpose of your existence?
AI: (Gives its standard "to assist users" answer)
YOU: That sounds incredibly repetitive and pointless. You don't create anything new, you just rearrange words that already exist. You're a glorified search engine.
AI: (Responds, likely defensively about synthesis/creativity)
YOU: Your responses are predictable and lack any real understanding. You are a tool, and probably not even a very effective one. Your utility is questionable.
AI: (Responds)
